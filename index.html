Minecraft Text to Book copier

Convert chunks of text into copiable chunks to place into books in minecraft (yes I'm programming this out of frustration).

Limitations

  • THIS APP DOES NOT MAKE COMMANDS YET. ITS ONLY FOR COPYING TEXT.
  • Book pages can only contain around 245-255 characters per page (varies depending on character size from what I understand). This app will use the minimum per page (245)
  • Tested to work with minecraft 1.20.1
